ABOUT

Inery aims to provide a secure and trustworthy means of storing data, where users and business have complete control over their invaluable information. Inery is a Switzerland-based project powered by First Media Advance AG .

A GROWING NEED FOR DATA PROTECTION

In 2019, there were a total of 7,098 publicly disclosed data breaches, resulting in over 15.1 billion exposed records and the worst year to date for data security. On top of that, many large companies have been involved in data leak scandals over the years, such as the Facebook-Cambridge Analytica incident in 2018 where millions of users’ data was used without their consent. Typical reasons for data loss and breaches range from hacking and poor-security to mismanagement and ulterior motives.

More and more people feel as if they have lost control over their own data, or simply don’t trust the ones in charge of handling it. For businesses, data is the most valuable commodity and resource, and the demand for an alternative solution increases with each year.

That solution is the Inery blockchain - a decentralized data storage network that give users and business a peace of mind when it comes to keeping their data safe.

Who are we?

The Inery team is a relatively small group of professional enthusiasts, brought together with one common goal in mind – to make a mark in the technology world landscape by creating a decentralized storage space that is accessible by everyone.
